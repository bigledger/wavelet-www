Best Practices

Dashboard Design Principles

  1. Focus on Action: Every metric should drive a decision
  2. Progressive Disclosure: Summary view with drill-down capability
  3. Context Matters: Show comparisons and trends, not just numbers
  4. Visual Hierarchy: Most important metrics prominently displayed
  5. Consistency: Standardized color coding and terminology
