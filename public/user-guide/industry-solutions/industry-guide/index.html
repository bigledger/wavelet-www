2. Diversity and Richness of Akaun Application Portfolio﻿

Wavelet handles the complexities of the ERP system, CRM, Point of Sales, Financial Accounting, Warehouse Management, Logistics and Delivery, Contact Centre, Digital Marketing, Inventory Management, Membership Program and various other business applications, and convert them into manageable applications (known as “applets”). Businesses are able to mix and match various applets tailored to their needs without purchasing a huge and complex system. The Data Lake serverless database allows massive data to be stored and processed centrally, functioning as the data exchange across multiple systems.
